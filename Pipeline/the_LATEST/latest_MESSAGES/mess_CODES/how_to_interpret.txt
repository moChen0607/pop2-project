Codes: MYA (maya), AIE (platform independent), NUK (nuke), HOU (houdini)
- 4 – Logger Level
- 1 - Section Type
- 2 - Secondary Descriptor
- 9 – Primary Iterator
- A – Secondary Iterator

Logger Level
9 being highest
- 1 to 2 – trace - 1 is windows, 2 is linux
- 3 - log
- 4 – debug
- 5 - info
- 6 – warning
- 7 - error
- 8 - exception
- 9 - critical

Section Type
- 1 – OS related
- 2 - Network Related
- 3 – Fix Attempt
- 4 – Thread related
- 5 - Exceeds/Too low/Quantity
- 6 - file related (generic)
- 7 - I/O (universal)
- 9 - misc
